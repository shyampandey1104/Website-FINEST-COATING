Frequently Asked Questions

Is bathtub refinishing messy? +

Well, that depends on who you hire! At Finest Coating, we carefully mask off the work area to protect your home. We make sure the work area looks as good after we're gone as it did when we arrived. We remove all masking paper and tape, haul away our trash, and leave your bathroom cleaner than we found it.

What does Finest Coating charge for bathtub refinishing? +

Every project is unique. The price depends on the amount of work needed, the condition of your bathtub, its size, and the color you choose. Other factors include any previous work done on the bathtub or past repairs. Contact us for a free, no-obligation estimate.

What is the expected lifespan of a refinishing project? +

The newly applied finish will last 5 to 10 years. The lifespan depends on the amount of daily use, wear, and tear, as well as proper maintenance and care.

How does your warranty work? +

We provide a 6-month warranty covering both labor and materials. A warranty is only as good as the company standing behind it, and with over 19 years of experience, we stand behind our work. Note: Our warranty does not cover accidental damage or failure to follow care and maintenance instructions.

Can you fix hollow-sounding bathtub floors? +

Yes! If your bathtub floor sounds hollow and feels unsupported, we can add a new cement foundation to provide proper support for bathing and showering.

Why Does My Bathtub Peel After Reglazing?

Understanding the common causes of peeling and how to prevent it

‚ö†Ô∏è The Main Problem: Improper Application

When a newly reglazed bathtub starts peeling, it's almost always due to improper preparation and application techniques. Professional reglazing, when done correctly, should last for 7-10 years with proper maintenance.

‚ùå Common Causes of Peeling

  • ‚Ä¢ Improper Surface Preparation: Not cleaning/etching properly
  • ‚Ä¢ Incorrect Primer Application: Wrong type or insufficient drying
  • ‚Ä¢ Poor Ventilation: Dust/debris settling on wet coating
  • ‚Ä¢ Low-Quality Materials: Using non-professional coatings
  • ‚Ä¢ Moisture Issues: Bathroom not fully dry before application

‚úÖ Professional Solution

  • ‚úì Deep Cleaning: Remove all soap scum and oils completely
  • ‚úì Proper Etching: Acid-based conditioner for proper adhesion
  • ‚úì Expert Application: Multiple thin coats, not one thick coat
  • ‚úì Quality Materials: Professional-grade epoxy coatings only
  • ‚úì Proper Curing: 24-48 hours before use
üí° Professional Tip: Always hire certified professionals with proven track records. DIY kits often lead to peeling within months. Our technicians follow strict protocols to ensure 10+ years of durability.
