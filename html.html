Our Professional Process

We follow a proven 5-step process to ensure perfect results every time

1

STEP 1: DEEP CLEANING

Thorough cleaning removes built-up grime and stains:

  • Scrub walls with abrasive cleanser and a stiff brush to lift soap scum and hard water deposits
  • For stubborn stains, apply gel stain remover and let dwell 15 minutes before scrubbing
  • Plug the drain and fill the tub with warm water and all-purpose cleaner. Let soak before scrubbing the entire tub surface
  • Rinse tub thoroughly removing all cleaner residues which can interfere with refinishing

Deep cleaning lets fresh refinishing coatings or paints adhere properly.

2

STEP 2: REPAIRING CHIPS AND CRACKS

Fix damage using bathtub repair kits:

  • Drain and fully dry the tub before repairs
  • Wide cracks need patching with waterproof wood filler along the entire crevice length
  • For chips, gently scrape out damaged areas into a "V" shape using a razor blade
  • Fill chips and scratches with adhesive epoxy, smoothing until level. Allow curing 24 hours
  • Use fine sandpaper to gently sand repairs flush with the surrounding tub surface

Seal damage properly before refinishing to prevent further deterioration.

3

STEP 3: SURFACE PREPARATION

We carefully prepare the surface using professional-grade materials to ensure maximum adhesion and durability of the new coating.

4

STEP 4: PROFESSIONAL COATING

Application of our premium coating system that provides a tough, durable, and stain-resistant finish. Available in multiple colors to match your bathroom design.

5

STEP 5: FINISHING & QUALITY CHECK

Final inspection and quality assurance. Your bathtub will be ready to use within 24 hours with a brilliant, smooth finish.
